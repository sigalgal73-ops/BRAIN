USE CASES

Use Cases – What Brain Does?

Brain is an AI-powered Co-Manager designed to move people, drive execution, and ensure that important things actually get done

🎯 Management & Execution
🔘Do Everything Button
  • Reviews everything you're responsible for and checks it's being handled
  • Analyzes goals, current state, gaps, risks, and opportunities
  • Produces a full execution plan: tasks, owners, WhatsApp messages, emails, and follow-ups
  • Defines success metrics and what needs to be reviewed next week
  • Brings things you haven't thought of — what people in your role typically miss
🗣️Conversational Leadership Partner
  • Morning conversation as a personal manager — starts with personal life: relationships, family, energy
  • Reviews yesterday's key win, today's biggest risk, and today's biggest opportunity
  • Presents a cockpit of topics and leads the conversation on what matters most, one question at a time
  • Ends with one key decision and priority list for the day
  • Available in the car — on the way to work, anytime
⏸️Stop & Think — Breaking Autopilot
  • Stops you 2–3 times a day for conscious thinking instead of working on autopilot
  • Reminds you not to open emails and WhatsApp before setting priorities
  • Stops managers before field visits and meetings for preliminary analysis
  • Asks: What's the goal? What's the problem? What are the options? What's the decision?
  • Checks whether you're reacting to events or managing them proactively
📋Planning, Goals & Execution
  • Builds multi-year, annual, quarterly, and monthly work plans with milestones
  • Ensures people connect to the plan and are motivated to execute
  • Measures daily: target achievement rates, gaps, risks, and blockers
  • Deploys work plans to the field with ongoing monitoring
  • Continuous follow-up until full completion with corrective actions
🏗️Personal + Work Control Cockpit
  • Shows everything important in personal and professional life in one place
  • Dedicates 50–80% of the screen to "how" — not just "what" — unlike regular BI
  • Tracks impact metrics: energy, openness, innovation, managerial courage, learning
  • Moves between control tower (big picture) and deep dive on specific topics
  • A new management category — not a dashboard, but an action manager
✅Improving WhatsApp Response Rates
  • Explains why responding to work messages is a professional obligation, not a choice
  • Builds commitment to respond and demands follow-through
  • Tracks and ensures sustained improvement in response rates over time
🔄 Change Management & Implementation
🚀Change Management & Process Implementation
  • Drives adoption of important initiatives, values, and behaviors
  • Runs long-term implementation campaigns
  • Creates alignment around new processes
  • Measures both implementation and business outcomes
⚡Organizational Activation
  • Increases urgency when organizations become complacent
  • Connects change initiatives to business logic and ROI
  • Demonstrates consequences of inaction through simulations
🌍Cultural Adaptation
  • Adapts communication style to different countries and cultures
  • Supports North America, Europe, Asia, and local market nuances
💡Persuasion Engine
  • Persuades through logic and personal benefit (WIIFM)
  • Encourages discussion and alternative viewpoints
  • Escalates when necessary and tracks execution status
🌱Initiative & Leadership Development
  • Encourages ownership and accountability
  • Develops proactive leadership behaviors
  • Helps employees become leaders rather than followers
👥 People, Engagement & Wellbeing
🤝Engagement & Connection
  • Replaces annual engagement surveys with a continuous process of ~50 measurement points throughout the year
  • Connects to mission, meaning, direct manager, team, goals, and innovation
  • Strengthens pride in support departments: admin, finance, IT, procurement
  • Gives managers a live status view: who's connected, why, and what to do about it
  • Based on stories and examples — not slogans and posters
🔋Energy, Motivation & Anti-Burnout
  • Increases energy, enthusiasm, and drive in employees and managers
  • Detects burnout, fatigue, and dropping morale early
  • Builds a regular routine for maintaining energy: morning, midday, and end of day
  • Activates recovery programs during periods of difficulty, stress, and crisis
  • Tracks impact metrics: energy, resilience, engagement, enthusiasm, and initiative
💪Resilience Development
  • Teaches how to build resilience against pressure, overload, change, failure, and criticism
  • Identifies where the employee breaks: energy, confidence, overload, fear, or burnout
  • Gives managers tools to strengthen correctly: what to say, when to encourage, when to demand
  • Measures resilience over time and shows numerical improvement (e.g., from 5 to 8)
🏃Employee Retention & Recruitment
  • Accompanies employees from day one — onboarding, team connection, training
  • Detects signs of burnout, disengagement, or resignation risk
  • Builds a personal retention plan for each employee
  • Helps recruit: defines why people should specifically join us
  • Results: Retention improvement from 12 to 24 months, 30–50% reduction in turnover
⚖️WLB, Wellness & Quality of Life
  • Checks that people get home at normal hours and builds a weekly plan: home, relationships, kids
  • Monitors sleep, nutrition, exercise, and mood
  • Supports through crises: work, relationships, money, health
  • Helps with personal financial management and future career planning
  • Brings tips for relationship and parenting development
🏆Improving Manager-Employee Relations
  • Teaches what to say, when, and how — to build trust, motivation, and cooperation
  • Teaches what not to say or do — to avoid damage and resistance
  • Prepares for difficult conversations, feedback sessions, and conflicts
  • Teaches how to receive criticism professionally and constructively
  • Measures improvement in relationships and communication quality over time
📚 Learning, Training & Knowledge
🎓Learning & Training Platform
  • Uses human-like AI avatars
  • Creates training programs in seconds
  • Delivers and tracks learning automatically
  • Sends micro-learning content throughout the day
  • Supports video, text, image, and avatar-based communication
🧠Organizational Knowledge System
  • Creates industry-specific knowledge libraries
  • Preserves organizational know-how
  • Stores processes, checklists, and best practices
  • Learns which persuasion and implementation strategies work best
📊 Measurement, Analytics & Innovation
📈Measurement & Organizational Insights
  • Measures factors most organizations never track
  • Employee engagement, trust, and psychological safety
  • Resilience, enthusiasm, and retention indicators
  • Execution activities that drive results
🔍Gap Analysis & Blind Spot Detection
  • Compares current performance to global best practices
  • Identifies missing opportunities and analyzes resistance
  • Recommends practical solutions
🔧Innovation Management
  • Provides innovation opportunity maps
  • Encourages continuous improvement daily
  • Identifies teams and individuals who are not innovating
💼 Sales, Service & Customers
📊Sales Performance Improvement
  • Connects employees to the importance of sales, targets, and their personal contribution
  • Checks daily target achievement — every employee and manager knows their numbers
  • Improves conversion rates, upsell, and complementary sales
  • Supports new customer acquisition and leads from existing customers
  • Results: Conversion improvement 10–30%, upsell growth 15–40%
📦Product Expertise
  • Builds a full expertise card for each product: benefits, FAQ, and sales arguments
  • Defines needs-assessment questions for each customer segment
  • Compares against competitors and identifies growth-leading products
  • Measures professional knowledge level and tracks improvement
  • Results: Professionalism improvement 20–50%, shorter onboarding time
⭐Service Excellence
  • Creates empathy for the customer: "How would you want someone to treat your father?"
  • Improves tone, body language, listening, courtesy, and phone quality
  • Provides opening lines, connection phrases, and customer approach frameworks
  • Delivers micro-training and conversation practice until embedded
  • Principle: "Treat customers the way you'd want the people you love most to be treated"
❤️Customer Retention & Loyalty
  • Analyzes customer files: consumption profile, frequency, basket, and activity drops
  • Detects at-risk customers and alerts the manager before they disappear
  • Activates smart loyalty programs: segments, personalized offers, birthdays, communities
  • Builds ambassador communities from loyal customers
  • Measures: returning customers, basket growth, churn reduction, referrals
🏆B2B Sales Excellence
  • Boosts sales energy through short routines, daily targets, encouragement, and focus
  • Strengthens relationship with decision-makers: trust, interests, personal and organizational value
  • Provides materials, arguments, differentiation, and objection responses
  • Manages Pareto of sales points: where the potential is, where to invest
  • Prepares for holidays: inventory, promotions, messages, and preferred customers
💰Cost Reduction & Efficiency
  • Builds savings projects: what's saved, who's responsible, how much, by when
  • Identifies waste: procurement, inventory, headcount, time, processes, and duplications
  • Tracks that the project is actually moving — not just sitting in a presentation
  • Surfaces exceptions: where savings are stuck and what needs to move now
  • Measures: savings percentage, money saved, unit cost reduction, profitability improvement
🌟 Future Generation
🔭Talent Identification & Retention
  • Identifies and flags high-potential "future generation" employees
  • Ensures every employee knows they're on a dedicated development and retention track
  • Detects resignation risk and readiness for promotion
  • Provides managers with an updated status view at all times
📈Personal Development Track
  • Builds a development plan for each employee: training, mentoring, and leadership assignments
  • Conducts periodic career conversations to understand aspirations
  • Exposes to strategic projects and senior management
  • Quarterly tracking: progress, satisfaction, resignation risk, and promotion readiness
🏗️Succession Planning
  • Builds a clear succession plan for every key role: who's the replacement and how ready are they
  • Ensures every critical role has at least one ready replacement
  • Measures readiness to replace key roles in real time
🛡️ Safety, Collaboration & Culture
⛑️Safety Culture
  • Connects people to safety through personal meaning: children, grandchildren, family, future
  • Creates daily active awareness — not a one-time training event
  • Teaches employees to identify 10 danger points in every work environment
  • Activates "laser focus": breathing, standing straight, slowing down, sharp environmental awareness
  • Strengthens near-miss reporting without punishment — reporting = professionalism
  • Measures: awareness, focus, alertness, courage to speak up, personal accountability
🤜Cross-Department Collaboration
  • Clearly defines who updates whom, when, and through which channel
  • Builds regular inter-department routines: meetings, coordination, joint problem-solving
  • Identifies barriers: lack of communication, trust gaps, or conflicting goals
  • Ensures cross-departmental decisions are followed through with tracking and follow-up
  • Results: 10–30% reduction in process times, 20–50% fewer coordination failures
🚀Innovation Management
  • Builds an organizational innovation map with dozens of action directions
  • Stops employees for proactive thinking and asks: what's frustrating you? what wastes time?
  • Measures who raises ideas and who doesn't — daily and individually
  • Creates a repeatable process: idea → experiment → measurement → decision → implementation
  • Results: Increase in implemented initiatives, shift from reactive to proactive organization
