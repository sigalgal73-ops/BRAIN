The science of human change

Persuasion · Follow-Up · Implementation — turning behavioral change from an art into a measurable science.

Brain began with a simple observation: most organizations don’t fail for lack of goals, procedures, training, dashboards or knowledge — they fail because desired behaviors aren’t consistently adopted, executed and sustained. This implementation gap exists across nearly every human environment. Brain studies it scientifically — across retail, banking, healthcare, education, military, senior communities and families — to discover the laws of persuasion, follow-up and behavioral implementation, and turn behavioral change from an art into a measurable, predictive science.
