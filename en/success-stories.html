Steimatzky
Book & Gift Retail Chain
+27%
Objective

Improve sales of checkout products — small impulse items placed next to the cashier.

The Problem Identified
  • Cashiers had no ready-to-use sales phrases for checkout items
  • Without clear phrases, confidence to offer products was low
  • Thousands of potential micro-sales were lost every day
  • No measurement of who suggested products and who did not
The Solution
  • Ready-to-use sales sentences placed directly at the checkout
  • Additional prompts placed beneath the checkout area for quick reference
  • Measurement system tracking who suggests products, who does not, and where gaps exist
  • Managers received clear visibility into performance differences between employees and branches
Result: 27% increase in checkout product sales — not from changing the products, but from changing employee behavior.

Soltam
Cookware Retail Chain
Objective

Improve the connection between regional managers and store managers, and strengthen alignment with sales targets and operational control.

Problems Identified
  • No structured method for regional managers to support store managers effectively
  • Missing upsell sales sentences and loyalty club recruitment scripts
  • No clear scripts to convert new customers
  • Lack of a control dashboard for regional managers to track store performance
The Solution
  • Complete operational playbook for regional managers
  • Sales scripts for upselling, loyalty club recruitment, and new customer acquisition
  • Store traffic-generation activity list
  • Control dashboard monitoring all critical performance indicators
Result: The entire sales network became energized. Regional managers gained focused direction. Store managers received practical support rather than abstract guidance.

Ormat
Industrial Energy Company
Objective

Improve workplace safety and reduce near-miss incidents in industrial environments.

Problems Identified
  • Workers lacked regular mental "reset moments" during the day
  • No structured pause moments before performing risky actions
  • Employees missing emotional awareness signals for fatigue, stress, and difficult situations
  • Workers did not consistently connect safety behavior to what matters most — their families
The Solution
  • Short video messages distributed across the factory
  • Micro-moments encouraging workers to pause before risky actions
  • Emotional reminders connecting safety to family and loved ones
  • Measurement systems for tracking near-miss incidents
Result: Safety awareness increased across the factory. Continuous behavioral reinforcement through video. Measurable reduction in near-miss incidents.

By The Way
Café & Restaurant Chain
Objective

Strengthen brand connection, employee retention, professional service quality, and upselling at the cashier.

Problems Identified
  • Employees lacked clear upselling sentences for menu items
  • Workers did not understand the personal benefit of selling more
  • No measurement of upsell attempts — daily, by employee, by branch
  • Managers lacked confidence to demand higher performance standards
The Solution
  • Upsell sales scripts for menu items
  • Messaging explaining the personal benefit of selling more
  • Daily measurement of upsell attempts by employee
  • Manager coaching to raise expectations and accountability
  • Continuous reinforcement: motivational videos, inspirational images, short clips
Result: Employees became more confident in upselling. Managers became more active in leading performance. Stores began achieving and frequently exceeding sales targets.

Fujitsu / Electrolux / AEG
Technology Retail Line
Objective

Improve professional selling of technological products in retail stores.

Problems Identified
  • Sales staff lacked professional product explanations
  • Sales activity not consistently guided by data analysis and category management
  • Many employees operated on automatic behavior rather than intentional selling
  • Energy levels and enthusiasm among employees were inconsistent
The Solution
  • Professional product explanation sentences for technological items
  • Structured retail category management based on sales data
  • Behavioral reset moments encouraging intentional rather than automatic selling
  • Motivational video clips to increase enthusiasm and employee energy
Result: Sales teams became more professional. Store energy increased. Product knowledge improved. Sales engagement became more active and focused.
