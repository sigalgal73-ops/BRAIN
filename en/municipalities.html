How Brain Works

Four core engines that turn intention into action

🧠
Brain Mentor
A personal AI mentor that speaks with the user, listens, asks thoughtful questions, and gently encourages healthy and meaningful daily actions. Over time, Brain becomes a trusted daily companion.
💬
Brain Persuader
A caring AI voice that uses gentle persuasion to encourage movement, social connection, and city participation. Speaks with care, encouragement, and respect — helping retirees take small actions that create big results.
🔄
Brain Follow-Upper
Continuously checks in to encourage action and track progress. Gently reminds, motivates, and supports until healthy activities actually happen. Monitors responses, adapts encouragement, and keeps the conversation positive and personal.
🎬
Brain Campaigner
A daily video campaign system. Each day, Brain sends a short engaging video that combines encouragement, simple explanations, and practical suggestions. Over time, builds awareness, shifts habits, and strengthens personal motivation.
